Namco System ES1

Specifications

  • CPU: Intel Core 2 Duo E8400
  • GPU: NVIDIA GeForce 9600 GT
  • Video Output:
    • 1 DVI-I, 1 VGA, 1 HDMI
    • 2 DVI
  • Storage:
    • Seagate Barracuda 7200.12 160 GB (ST3160318AS)
    • Hitachi Deskstar 7K1000.C 160 GB (HDS721016CLA382)
  • Operating System: arcadelinux (Debian 4.0 kernel based) x86
  • Audio: Integrated HDA (5.1 ch)
  • Security:
    • TPM 1.2
    • HDD write protection
    • HASP HL Max USB dongle
